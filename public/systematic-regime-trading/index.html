Architecture

190 Stocks → 6 Features → 5 Models → Ensemble → Signal → Backtest
  • HMM (5-state Gaussian, remapped to 3): transition persistence
  • GARCH(1,1) Student-t: conditional volatility clustering
  • K-Means: feature-space clustering
  • Gaussian Mixture Model: Bayesian posterior probabilities
  • Markov-Switching (Hamilton 1989): regime-dependent variance

Walk-forward validation, expanding window, quarterly refit. 59 windows, 3,771 out-of-sample predictions. SPY benchmark. 1-day execution lag. Asymmetric confirmation filters.
